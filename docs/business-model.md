# Business Model

## Primary Revenue — SaaS Subscription

### Free Tier

- Basic listing
- Limited monthly bookings
- Self-serve funnel into paid plans

### Paid Tier (Indicative)

- Solo: ~99–149 PLN/month
- Small teams: ~249–399 PLN/month
- Includes QR check-in, analytics, multiple staff, SMS reminders, unlimited bookings

### One-Time Setup Fee

- 300–500 PLN for onboarding/implementation
- Purpose: increase commitment quality and retention

## Secondary Revenue Streams

1. **Transaction fee:** 1–2% on processed payments
2. **SMS bundles:** notification credits sold separately
3. **Featured/verified listing:** ~49 PLN/month for non-platform schools
4. **White-label license:** annual contracts for larger chains

## Monetization Principles

- Free tier drives adoption and data flywheel.
- Paid value tied to operational outcomes (bookings, attendance, reminders).
- Multiple low-friction upsells reduce dependence on one revenue source.

## Tracking KPIs

- MRR / ARPU
- Setup fee attach rate
- SMS bundle take rate
- Payment GMV and fee revenue
- Featured listing conversion rate
