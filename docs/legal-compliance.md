# Legal & Compliance Baseline

## Data & Content Rules

- Public business information (name, address, hours, categories) may be listed.
- Personal data handling must follow GDPR obligations.
- Do not publish school photos without explicit permission.

## Mandatory Launch Items

1. Cookie notice with GDPR-compliant consent handling
2. Privacy policy
3. Takedown / report incorrect information flow
4. Public contact email for legal/content correction requests

## Directory Safety Rules

- Unclaimed listings stay public but can be corrected or challenged.
- Add `Report incorrect information` on each detail page and footer.
- Process correction requests with timestamped audit notes.

## Operational Policy

- Define SLA for correction/takedown requests.
- Assign owner for legal inbox triage.
- Keep evidence trail of source, edits, and requests.

## Note

This is an operational baseline and not legal advice. Validate final policies and wording with qualified legal counsel before public launch.
